<h2><u>Student Leaders Investiture&nbsp;2025</u></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 75%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Student Leaders/Stud_Lead_Inv_2026__7_.jpg">

<p>On 14 July 2025, Townsville Primary School proudly held the <strong>Student Leaders Investiture</strong>,
welcoming our new P4 student leaders into the school community and formally
introducing the members of the Student Leader Council. The ceremony marked
a special milestone, as each leader received a collar pin and certificate,
symbolizing the start of their exciting journey. It was a moment of pride,
responsibility, and inspiration for both the leaders and the Townsville
family!</p>
