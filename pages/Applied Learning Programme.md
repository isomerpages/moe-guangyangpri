<p>Townsville Primary School’s <strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</strong>, <em>“iDARE2 to be a Critical, Adaptive and Inventive Thinker,”</em> nurtures
students who can think critically, adapt to evolving circumstances, and
generate creative solutions to real-world challenges.</p>

<p>Through our <strong>Curriculum Infusion lessons</strong> and the <strong>Interdisciplinary Project Work (Project IGNITE)</strong> framework,
students apply their learning across disciplines to address authentic issues.
These experiences empower them to become reflective, self-directed and
future-ready learners.</p>
