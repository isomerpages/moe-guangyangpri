<p>At Townsville, we proudly embody our values of <strong>Diligence, Aspiration, Respect</strong>,
and <strong>Empathy </strong>through inclusive initiatives supporting Persons
with Disabilities (PWDs), aligning with Singapore's Purple Parade movement.</p>
<p>Our students demonstrated empathy by pledging personal statements to understand
and include others with different abilities.</p>
<p>Our parents' support group created immersive tactile booths, allowing
students to step into the shoes of differently abled individuals while
our staff showed solidarity and wore purple on Children's Day, embracing
the CARE principles of Compassion, Awareness, Respect, and Engagement.</p>
<p>We also partnered with the Singapore Association of the Visually Handicapped,
engaging their mobile massage services and the Down Syndrome Association
and Crunchy Teeth, a ground-up initiative supporting Singapore's autistic
community, with a merchandise booth during our staff retail therapy event.</p>
<p>Through these efforts, we foster a culture of inclusivity, empathy, and
understanding, making a positive impact on our school community!</p>
