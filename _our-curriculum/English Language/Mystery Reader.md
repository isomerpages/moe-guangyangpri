<p><strong>Mystery Reader</strong> is an exciting programme where students
gather in the library to enjoy a story but the identity of the reader remains
a surprise! Teachers take turns reading aloud, sharing engaging stories
and bringing characters to life.</p>
<p>This fun and interactive activity not only <strong>fosters a love for reading,</strong> but
also encourages students to listen attentively, make predictions and enjoy
the thrill of discovering who the next reader will be.</p>
