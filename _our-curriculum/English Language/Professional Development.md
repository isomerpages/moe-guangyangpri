Through focused professional discourse, they engage in level-specific discussions aimed at refining their competencies and addressing concerns. The collaborative environment extends to cooperative learning activities, including innovative approaches such as 'Stand-up, Hand up, Pair up.' This interactive method facilitates knowledge exchange and peer-to-peer learning.
