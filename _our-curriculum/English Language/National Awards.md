<p>We are also proud to be recognised for our innovative approaches to teaching
and learning. We are honoured to have received the Innergy Award for Innovation,
celebrating our commitment to nurturing confident, curious, and capable
learners. This award is awarded to individuals who have been instrumental
in developing and implementing innovative approaches that have improved
teaching and learning and/or work processes in their workplaces.</p>
