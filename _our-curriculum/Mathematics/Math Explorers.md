<h2><strong><u>Math Explorers (P1-P5)</u></strong></h2>
<p>From the moment we wake up until we go to bed, we use mathematical skills
without even realizing it. With that in mind, the activities were designed
to help students apply the mathematical concepts and skills they learn
in the classroom to real-life situations.
<br>Math Explorers tasks are specially created to engage students in reasoning,
critical thinking, and communication within authentic, real-world contexts.
These tasks encourage students to explore their school environment and
learning experiences, helping them make meaningful mathematical connections
to the world around them.</p>
<p> P<strong>1:</strong> Bingo game with 9 activities around the school to
explore and apply mathematical ideas.</p>
<p> P<strong>2:</strong> Problem-solving tasks on measuring lengths and planning
food orders within a given budget.</p>
<p> P<strong>3:</strong> Understanding the canteen layout and practicing
budgeting skills.</p>
<p> P<strong>4:</strong> Reading and interpreting NAPFA (National Physical
Fitness Award) tables to set and improve personal fitness goals.</p>
<p> P<strong>5:</strong> Planning and budgeting for the National Education
(NE) goodie kit.</p>
