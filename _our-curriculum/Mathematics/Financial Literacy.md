<h2><strong><u>Financial Literacy</u></strong></h2>
<p>At Townsville, we believe it is never too early to begin learning about
money management. Through engaging assembly programmes, skits, and information
board activities, pupils are introduced to fundamental financial principles
as well as the evolving landscape of digital payments and financial technologies.</p>
