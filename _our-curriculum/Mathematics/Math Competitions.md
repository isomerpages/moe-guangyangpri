<h2><strong>2025 Math Competitions</strong></h2>
<p>Townsvillites from P4 to P6 have the opportunity to participate in various
national-level mathematics competitions, aimed at nurturing and recognising
budding talents in primary schools.</p>
<p>In 2025, we celebrated our students’ accomplishments with pride, acknowledging
their hard work, perseverance, and growth through various mathematics competitions.</p>
<p>• <strong>Raffles Mathematics Olympiad (RMO)</strong>
<br>Nine students participated, achieving 1 Distinction and 8 Merit awards.</p>
<p>• <strong>National Mathematical Olympiad of Singapore (NMOS)</strong>
<br>Twenty students participated, with 12 students receiving awards: 1 Gold,
1 Silver, 6 Bronze, and 4 Honourable Mentions. Townsville also achieved
a remarkable milestone by ranking among the Top 20 schools in overall team
performance.</p>
<p>• <strong>International Achievements</strong>
<br>Li Yinxi (5E2) was selected to represent Singapore at the 22nd International
Mathematics and Science Olympiad (IMSO 2025), held from 5–9 October in
Kedah, Malaysia, where he attained a Gold award.
<br>In addition, Yinxi received a Gold award at the Asia Pacific Mathematical
Olympiad for Primary Schools (APMOPS).</p>
<h4><strong>Well done to our students!</strong></h4>
