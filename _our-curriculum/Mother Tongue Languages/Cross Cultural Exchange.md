<p><strong>Cultural Immersion Beyond Borders</strong>
</p>
<p>Townsville Primary School hosted a delegation of students from Shenzhen
OCT Primary School, for a vibrant cultural exchange. The visit was successfully
integrated into our annual Mother Tongue Language Fortnight (MTLF), offering
our guests a deep dive into the diverse heritage of Singapore.</p>
<p>Our Malay Language (ML) and Tamil Language (TL) pupils enthusiastically
guided the visitors through various traditional activities. The exchange
included hands-on cultural crafts such as the intricate Batik Painting
and Karagam Making. Students also participated in spirited traditional
games, including the energetic Sepak Takraw and Tarik Upih, alongside Indian
Pinyata and the Balancing Dance (Karagaattam).</p>
<p>Through these immersive activities, both our local pupils and our guests
gained a deeper understanding of Singapore's rich cultural tapestry, demonstrating
how our international partnerships enrich the learning journey and broaden
the perspectives of all our students.</p>
