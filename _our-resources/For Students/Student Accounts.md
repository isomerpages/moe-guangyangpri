<h1>Student MOE Identity Management System (MIMS)</h1>
<p></p>
<p>MIMS is the primary mode for students to log in to MOE ICT tools. School
Laptops, Student iCON and Student Learning Space (SLS) now require a MIMS
account to log in.</p>
